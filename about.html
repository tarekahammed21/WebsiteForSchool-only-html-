About Us:

Narayanganj Model School mission is to develop curious, knowledgeable and caring young individuals, who will be equipped to tackle head-on the challenges of our modern-day "global village." They will aspire to become responsible and productive citizens, who will contribute to their communities, and embrace and respect people from other cultures and walks of life.
